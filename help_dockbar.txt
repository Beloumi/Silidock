# help

Click the wheel button (top left) to add and remove programs to and from the dockbar; 
you will find instructions there. 

Click the X-button (top right) to close this program. Invoked programs will remain. 

Click pliers button (below wheel button) to make configurations like size, position 
and columns of this menubar, date display. 
To apply modifications click the reload button (below help button). 
If you change the position on screen, you have to click reload button two times.
